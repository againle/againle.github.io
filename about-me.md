# About Me

I've been fortunate to learn from different cities, cultures, and landscapes. The diversity of the world keeps me curious — about the deep structures of nature, and about the beauty of human connection. I believe in an open, kind, and sincere world — one where we respect each other not as instruments, but as ends in ourselves.
