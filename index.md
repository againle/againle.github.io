I am **Hongkai Liu**, a Mathematics and Computer Science double major at **The University of Hong Kong**. I work at the intersection of theory and practice—from mathematical foundations to deployed AI systems.

My research explores how learning systems acquire and represent knowledge, what mathematical structures underlie modern AI, and how we can build reliable, interpretable, and impactful AI systems.

I approach these questions through **optimization theory**, **statistical learning**, **foundation model architecture**, and **real-world deployment**—spanning AI research, quantitative finance, and social impact.
